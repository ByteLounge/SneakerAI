# SneakerAI SDG Alignment Protocol

This document outlines how SneakerAI directly contributes to the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), promoting a greener, more responsible secondary retail market.

## 🏭 Goal 9: Industry, Innovation and Infrastructure
SneakerAI modernizes the volatile resale market by introducing an open-access AI intelligence layer. By predicting trends rather than relying purely on FOMO (Fear Of Missing Out), we provide equitable data infrastructure to both small independent retailers and enterprise-level consolidators. 

## 🔄 Goal 12: Responsible Consumption and Production
The fast-fashion cycle creates massive textile waste. High-end sneakers are often over-consumed or counterfeited. SneakerAI encourages the **circular economy**. 
- Our platform helps price secondary items accurately, increasing the likelihood they are resold instead of thrown away. 
- The *Eco Score* module holds brands accountable for their manufacturing practices, heavily favoring initiatives like Nike's "Move to Zero" and Adidas' "Ocean Plastic".

## 🌍 Goal 13: Climate Action
SneakerAI includes a built-in carbon offset estimator for the resale market. Purchasing a pair of sneakers on the secondary market instead of demanding new retail production saves an estimated 14kg of CO² per pair and roughly 3,500 gallons of water.

## 🤝 Goal 8: Decent Work and Economic Growth
By formalizing the secondary sneaker market with accurate AI financial tools, independent resellers can achieve stable, predictable economic growth without being exploited by opaque marketplace fees. We lower the barrier to entry for micro-entrepreneurs worldwide.
